BOOK LENDING


Welcome to our Book Lending Service, where library members can borrow books and other materials with ease. Our goal is to provide a seamless borrowing experience, ensuring access to knowledge and entertainment for all our users.

How It Works

  1. Browse our online catalog or visit the library to find the book you want.

  2. Check the book's availability.

  3. Log in to your account and reserve the book online or pick it up at the library.

  4. Borrow the book using your library card at the self-checkout kiosk or the circulation desk.

  5. Return the book by the due date to avoid late fees.

Borrowing Rules & Policies

  • Membership Requirement: Only registered library members can borrow books.

  • Loan Period: Books can be borrowed for 14-30 days, depending on the book category.

  • Renewals: Members can renew books twice, provided there are no pending reservations.

  • Borrowing Limit: Members can borrow up to 5 books at a time.

  • Late Fees: Overdue books incur a ₹ 5.00 per day fine.

  • Lost or Damaged Books: Members are responsible for replacing lost or damaged books.

Book Return Process

  1. Return books at the circulation desk or drop box.

  2. Ensure books are returned in good condition.

  3. Renew online if you need more time.

Digital & E-Book Lending

  • Access thousands of e-books and audiobooks.

  • Borrow digital books through our website.

  • Enjoy a 14-day digital loan period, auto-return enabled.
